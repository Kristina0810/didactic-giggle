## Content Production Plan

### Tools & Infrastructure

**Video Production**:
- Screen recording: OBS Studio
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve
- Platform: Vimeo (private hosting) or Teachable
- Thumbnail design: Canva Pro

**Course Platform Options**:
1. **Teachable** (Recommended)
   - Pros: Easy, professional, built-in payments
   - Cons: 5% transaction fee (free plan)
   - Cost: $0-59/month

2. **Podia**
   - Pros: No transaction fees, includes email
   - Cons: $39/month minimum
   - Cost: $39-89/month

3. **Self-hosted (WordPress + LearnDash)**
   - Pros: Full control, no fees
   - Cons: Setup complexity, maintenance
   - Cost: ~$300/year

**Recommendation**: Start with Teachable, migrate to self-hosted at 500+ students
