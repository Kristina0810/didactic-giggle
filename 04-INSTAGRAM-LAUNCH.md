## Step 3: Growth Tactics (Weeks 3-6)

### 3.1 Hashtag Strategy

**Use 3 tiers of hashtags** (5-10 total per post):

**Tier 1: Niche** (10K-100K posts)
- #LoRAtraining
- #ComfyUI
- #AIcharacter
- #CharacterConsistency

**Tier 2: Category** (100K-1M posts)
- #AIartwork
- #DigitalCreator
- #ContentStrategy
- #AIinfluencer

**Tier 3: Broad** (1M-10M posts)
- #AIart
- #DigitalArt
- #ContentCreation

**Why 3 Tiers?**
- Niche: Easier to rank, highly targeted audience
- Category: Moderate competition, quality followers
- Broad: Hard to rank, but massive reach potential

**Pro Tip:** Research competitors' hashtags, find which ones they rank for, use those.

### 3.2 Engagement Strategy (1 hour/day)

**Daily Engagement Routine:**

**30 minutes before posting:**
1. Find 10 accounts in your niche (search hashtags)
2. Like their last 3 posts
3. Leave thoughtful comments (not "nice!" - ask questions, add value)
4. Follow if they align with your brand

**30 minutes after posting:**
1. Respond to ALL comments on your post (within 1 hour = algorithm boost)
2. Like every comment
3. Ask follow-up questions (encourages more comments)

**Result:** Algorithm sees high engagement â†’ shows your content to more people â†’ faster growth

### 3.3 Collaboration & Shoutouts

**Common Mistakes:**

1. **"Too expensive"** â†’ Offer payment plan ($49/month x 2)

4. **"I'm not tech-savvy"** â†’ Show behind-the-scenes, screen recordings (it's easier than they think)

**Fix:** Run Q&A sessions in stories, address objections head-on

### "I have followers but low engagement"

**Causes:**

3. **Boring captions** â†’ Use storytelling, emotion, vulnerability
